SCP ENIGMA

About Us


Reality Anchor Studios is an arising independent game development studio formed by a small team of talented artists and developers who are both industriously ardent and incredibly determined to produce their first upcoming project: SCP Enigma.


SCP: Enigma is a single-player survival horror set in the SCP universe. The player, a radio technician of Site-74's Armed Research and Containment facility, finds themselves plunged into the dark depths of the complex amid a catastrophic containment breach.


DISCLAIMER: The project is in super early development.

As a small team of passionate artists, developers and contributors (working freelance), we are working hard to introduce a LOT of awesome things in the future.

We're aiming for immersive worldbuilding, unique storytelling, new challenges (with new code, new environments, new composition actively implemented by the talented individuals of the team).

Support of any kind to the project is incredibly important to us, and will be forever appreciated! Thank you. :]



HISTORY(so far...)


Reality Anchor (formerly Crystal Blue Studios) was founded in 2022 by Lead Programmer and Developer Frames.


Moved by the developers' efforts and the project's artistry, the Reality Anchor team initially considered developing a remake of Aerie Gaming Studios' SCP: Unity (in itself a remake of SCP: Containment Breach), following its abrupt permanent cancellation back in 2021. The team was keen to produce a faithful rekindling of the work by resonating the game's beloved style, atmosphere and gameplay experience.


Reality Anchor eventually felt that they needed to pursue a significantly different direction of worldbuilding and narrative in order to effectively achieve a unique identity in the SCP media. By uniting their best skills and through their shared love of the SCP universe, this ultimately led to the team's creation of SCP: Enigma.


Some assets from SCP: Unity were sourced and used in the early developments of SCP: Enigma, with explicit permission from the artists who created them (many of whom presently work under the Aethon Collective team).
